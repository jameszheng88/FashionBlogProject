Fashion bloggers in their 30’s you should know about in 2023

Just like all good things in life – cheese and wine, for example – we believe fashion sense only improves with age. If you’ve been scrolling Instagram and chasing Outfit of the Day posts but nothing quite seems to be clicking anymore, you may have matured beyond the fashion influencers you’re currently following.

Each generation has its own style, and your generation is full of successful entrepreneurs, cool mums, and seasoned fashionistas… probably not unlike you! Here are ten influencers in their 30s who are sure to be your next style guru.

  1. Ellie Delphine
    • Age: 32
    • Location: France
    • Why you should follow her: With 147K followers on Instagram it’s no question that Ellie is an influencer to watch. This French fashionista loves bold colors and patterns and flowing silhouettes.
  2. Chriselle Lim
    • Age: 37
    • Location: Los Angeles, California
    • Why you should follow her: Chriselle Lim is no stranger to the fashion world. She founded her own fashion and lifestyle brand, The Chriselle Factor, has worked as a stylist and fashion editor, and is a veteran of the fashion week scene. An American fashion influencer, she has amassed 1.5 million followers on Instagram
  3. Brittany Xavier
    • Age: 35
    • Location: Los Angeles, California
    • Why you should follow her: Founder of the popular Thrifts and Threads blog, Brittany describes her style as “cool, comfortable, and a bit edgy”. She’s worked with brands like Dior, H&M, Marc Jacobs, and YSL. She might be a Millennial but her accounts are also wildly popular amongst the Gen Z crowd.
  4. Chiara Ferragni
    • Age: 35
    • Location: Milan, Italy
    • Why you should follow her: One of THE original influencers, Chiara got her start in fashion through her popular blog The Blonde Salad. She has since launched her own brand and even produced and starred in a docuseries about her life. Follow her for her bold, sexy and daring style.
